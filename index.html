5 Reasons to attend
ShipItCon 2017

Created by Thomas Shaw (Co-Founder of ShipItCon) / @tomwillfixit / @shipitcon

Location

Dublin has become a recognised centre of tech excellence. Tech giants of the world have chosen to make Dublin their home. In recent years there has been a surge in amazing Irish start-up companies and a tech community that includes some of the world’s best and brightest engineers.

From brilliant graduates to the cultural vibrancy, the scope of the city matches the scope of these companies. They’ve discovered what makes Dublin great and have made themselves a part of it.

Venue

The Round Room at Mansion House is a beautfiul venue, steeped in Irish history and situated in the heart of Dublin.

The Round Room was purpose designed in 1821 to receive King George IV. Remarkable political events have taken place here including the first ever meeting of the Dáil Eireann in 1919 and throughout the years we have proudly welcomed high profile guests from across the globe including Pope John Paul II, Nelson Mandela, Queen Victoria, Prince Rainier III and Princess Grace of Monaco.

People

We are putting together a world class lineup of speakers who will share their experiences of Continuous Delivery, Release Management and DevOps practices. ShipItCon is an event for people passionate about technology.

Everyone will be made to feel welcome at ShipItCon. This is a community event and we are fully committed to providing a harassment-free, welcoming, and inclusive experience for everyone involved; the speakers; the organisers; event staff; and for every individual attendee.

Passion

At ShipItCon we are passionate about technology and the advantages Continuous Delivery can provide to any size of organisation whichs delivers code. ShipItCon is a non-profit community event. The founders are volunteering their time to create an amazing, diverse, educational and fun event.

All money raised from sponsorship and ticket sales will be used to fund the event. Any excess money will be donated to a local good cause.

Knowledge sharing

As well as a wonderful line up of talks there will be opportunities to chat with thought leaders and industry innovators throughout the day. From experience it's often these conversations which reap the biggest benefits.

Chat with fellow engineers about their CI/CD processes, tooling, cultural challenges and more. Learn about the next generation of tooling from companies such as Cloudbees and Chef. You may even run into an old friend or two.
